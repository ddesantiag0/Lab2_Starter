Project Overview

The Terraform Initiative is set to revolutionize the way we approach sustainable urban development. The project will focus on creating an integrated platform for smart city management, incorporating AI to optimize resources and improve the lives of citizens.

Objectives & Key Results

  • Develop a scalable smart city platform by Q4 2024.
  • Onboard and integrate with at least three city infrastructures by Q1 2025.
  • Achieve a 20% increase in resource efficiency in pilot cities within six months of deployment.

Action Items

  • Emma will coordinate with city planners to establish requirements.
  • Liam will lead the technical architecture discovery phase.
  • Olivia will draft initial design concepts for the platform interface.
  • Noah will develop a testing strategy to ensure platform reliability and security.
  • Ava will create a marketing plan to engage stakeholders and the public.
