Project Overview

The Terraform Initiative aims to harness cutting-edge AI and IoT technologies to develop an integrated platform for managing urban environments sustainably. By optimizing resource allocation and enhancing public services, the initiative will significantly improve urban living conditions and pave the way for future smart cities.

Objectives & Key Results

  • Finalize the platform's technical architecture by Q3 2024.
  • Launch a pilot program in two metropolitan areas by Q2 2025.
  • Reduce energy consumption and waste production by 25% in pilot areas within one year.
  • Engage with at least 100,000 citizens through the platform by Q4 2025.
